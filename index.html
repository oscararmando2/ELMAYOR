Our Story

Where Heritage Meets Modern Craft

🌶

El Mayor was born from a deep love of Mexico's culinary heritage — the kind that lives in family kitchens, passed down in handwritten recipe books, and perfected over decades of tradition.

Our chefs source the finest fresh ingredients and pair time-honored techniques with contemporary presentation, creating a dining experience that honors the past while embracing the future.
